How to Play

          
        


        

          
Goal

          
Survive the dungeon. Win by clearing the deck. Lose if health reaches 0.


          
Cards

          
    
            
  • Monsters: ♣ and ♠, values 2-14.
    • 
            
  • Weapons: ♦ 2-10.
    • 
            
  • Potions: ♥ 2-10.
    • 
          


          
Each turn

          
    
            
  1. Draw until 4 cards are visible, unless the deck is nearly empty.
    2. 
            
  2. Either Avoid the room or Face it.
    3. 
            
  3. Select the cards you want to resolve, then press Face Room.
    4. 
            
  4. The remaining visible card carries into the next room.
    5. 
          


          
Important rules

          
    
            
  • No consecutive avoids.
    • 
            
  • Only 1 potion per room heals you. Extra potions are discarded with no effect.
    • 
            
  • 
              Weapon chain rule: once a weapon defeats a monster, it may only defeat later monsters with an
              equal or lower value. Example: if ♦7 defeats a 6, it can later defeat 6 or less, but not 8.
            
    • 
            
  • Equipping a new weapon discards the old weapon and its monster stack.
    • 
          


          
Scoring

          
    
            
  • Win: score = remaining health.
    • 
            
  • Loss: score = negative sum of monster values still left in the dungeon.
